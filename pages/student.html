PROJECT DESCRIPTION

This graduation project explores the intersection of digital media and traditional art forms, challenging conventional boundaries and creating new dialogues between past and present. Through a series of interactive installations and digital compositions, the work examines themes of memory, identity, and technological mediation.

The project draws inspiration from both historical art movements and contemporary digital practices, creating a hybrid aesthetic that reflects our current cultural moment. Each piece invites viewers to reconsider their relationship with technology and art, offering new perspectives on how we create and consume visual culture in the digital age.
